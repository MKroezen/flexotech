About Us

Flexotech, founded in 1994, produces and sells adhesives. We advise companies and offer solutions for many adhesive technology questions and problems. We also have experience with many production facilities where we work with different adhesive processing methods. Markets we serve include: Filters, Textiles, Packaging, Labeling, Wood, and some specialties.

Product Groups

  • Polyurethane: PU hotmelt, 100% PU, PU dispersions and 2K PU systems.
  • EVA's: Hotmelts and dispersions.
  • Polyolefine: Hotmelts.
  • Rubber: Hotmelts.
  • Epoxy: 2K systems.
  • PvaC: Dispersions 1 and 2K products.
